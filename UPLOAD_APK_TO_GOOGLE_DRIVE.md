## 📤 Method 1: Upload via Web Browser (Easiest)

### Step 1: Open Google Drive
1. Open your web browser (Chrome, Edge, Firefox, etc.)
2. Go to: **https://drive.google.com**
3. Sign in with your Google account

### Step 2: Upload the APK File
1. Click the **"New"** button (top left, blue button)
2. Select **"File upload"**
3. Navigate to: `C:\Users\koshi\cursor-apps\rider-mobile-pwa\android\app\build\outputs\apk\release\`
4. Select **`app-release.apk`** (or `rideapp-release.apk` if that's the filename)
5. Click **"Open"**
6. Wait for upload to complete (shows progress at bottom right)

### Step 3: Share the File
**Option A: Get Shareable Link (Recommended)**
1. Right-click on the uploaded APK file in Google Drive
2. Click **"Share"**
3. Click **"Change to anyone with the link"**
4. Set permission to **"Viewer"** (they can download)
5. Click **"Copy link"**
6. The link is now copied to your clipboard

**Option B: Share with Specific Email**
1. Right-click on the APK file
2. Click **"Share"**
3. Enter your phone's email address (or your own email)
4. Set permission to **"Viewer"**
5. Click **"Send"**

## 📲 Download on Your Phone

### Step 1: Open Google Drive on Phone
1. Open **Google Drive app** on your Android phone
   - If not installed, download from Play Store
2. Sign in with the same Google account

### Step 2: Find and Download APK
**If you shared via link:**
1. Open the link on your phone (from email, message, or browser)
2. Tap **"Download"** or the download icon
3. File will download to your phone

**If file is in your Drive:**
1. Open Google Drive app
2. Find `app-release.apk` in your files
3. Tap the file
4. Tap the **three dots menu** (⋮)
5. Tap **"Download"** or **"Make available offline"**

### Step 3: Install APK
1. Open **File Manager** app on your phone
2. Go to **Downloads** folder
3. Find `app-release.apk`
4. Tap the file
5. If prompted, enable **"Install from Unknown Sources"**
6. Tap **"Install"**
7. Wait for installation
8. Tap **"Open"** to launch the app

## 🚨 Troubleshooting

**Upload is slow:**
- Large file (66 MB) - be patient
- Check your internet connection
- Try during off-peak hours

**Can't find file on phone:**
- Make sure you're signed into same Google account
- Check "Shared with me" section in Drive app
- Refresh the Drive app

**Download fails:**
- Check phone storage space (need ~70 MB free)
- Try downloading via browser instead of app
- Check internet connection on phone

**Installation blocked:**
- Go to Settings → Security → Enable "Install from Unknown Sources"
- Or when installing, Android will prompt you to allow
