# Install APK from Email - Step by Step

**You've emailed the APK to yourself. Here's how to install it on your phone:**

---

## 📱 Step 1: Open Email on Your Phone

1. **Open your email app** on your Android phone
   - Gmail, Outlook, Yahoo Mail, or whatever email app you use

2. **Find the email** you just sent to yourself
   - Look for the email with `app-release.apk` attachment
   - Subject might be something like "FW: app-release.apk" or whatever you used

---

## 📥 Step 2: Download the APK

1. **Open the email** with the APK attachment

2. **Tap on the attachment** (`app-release.apk` or `rideapp-release.apk`)
   - You'll see a download icon or "Download" button

3. **Tap "Download"** or the download icon
   - File will start downloading
   - You'll see a notification: "Downloading app-release.apk"

4. **Wait for download to complete**
   - Check notification bar for "Download complete"
   - Or go to Downloads folder to verify

---

## 🔓 Step 3: Enable Unknown Sources (If Needed)

**Android will prompt you, but here's how to enable manually:**

1. **Go to Settings** on your phone

2. **Navigate to:**
   - **Settings → Security → Install unknown apps**
   - OR **Settings → Apps → Special access → Install unknown apps**
   - (Location varies by Android version)

3. **Select your email app** (Gmail, Outlook, etc.)

4. **Enable "Allow from this source"** or toggle it ON

**OR** - Android will prompt you automatically when you try to install, just tap "Allow" or "Settings" and enable it.

---

## 📲 Step 4: Install the APK

### Method A: From Email App
1. **Tap the downloaded attachment** in your email again
2. **Tap "Install"** when prompted
3. **Wait for installation** (shows progress)
4. **Tap "Open"** when done

### Method B: From File Manager
1. **Open File Manager** app on your phone
2. **Go to Downloads** folder
3. **Find `app-release.apk`**
4. **Tap the file**
5. **Tap "Install"**
6. **Wait for installation**
7. **Tap "Open"** or "Done"

---

## ✅ Step 5: Verify Installation

1. **Check app drawer** for "GlobApp Rider" app
   - Swipe up from home screen or tap app drawer icon

2. **Open the app**

3. **Test connection:**
   - App should connect to `https://globapp.org/api/v1`
   - Try logging in or using main features

## 🚨 Troubleshooting

### Email attachment too large?
- Some email providers limit attachments to 25MB
- Your APK is 66.3 MB - might not send via email
- **Solution:** Use Google Drive link instead (upload to Drive, share link via email)

### Can't download attachment?
- Check phone storage space (need ~70 MB free)
- Try opening email in browser instead of app
- Check internet connection

### Installation blocked?
- Go to Settings → Security → Install unknown apps
- Enable for your email app
- Try installing again

### App won't open?
- Check if installation completed successfully
- Try restarting phone
- Check app permissions in Settings → Apps
